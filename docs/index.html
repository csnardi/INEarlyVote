Updated: 2020-10-20

Total Early Votes: 734,814 (26.2% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 734,814 votes (78.6% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 374,464 in-person early votes (47.0% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 356,018 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 523,727 mail ballots.
