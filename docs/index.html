Indiana Early Voting Statistics

Updated: 2020-10-21

Total Early Votes: 798,307 (28.4% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 798,307 votes (85.4% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 423,812 in-person early votes (53.2% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 369,603 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 529,335 mail ballots.
