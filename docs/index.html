Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 1,828,424 votes (195.7% of all votes cast early in 2016).

Hoosiers have cast 1,320,481 in-person early votes (165.9% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Hoosiers have cast 495,581 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.
