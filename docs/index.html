Indiana Early Voting Statistics

Updated: 2020-10-22

Total Early Votes: 864,132 (30.8% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 864,132 votes (92.5% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 475,075 in-person early votes (59.7% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 383,712 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 534,665 mail ballots.
