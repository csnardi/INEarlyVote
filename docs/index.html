Total Early Votes: 931,514 (33.2% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 931,514 votes (99.7% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 527,863 in-person early votes (66.3% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 397,966 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 540,895 mail ballots.
