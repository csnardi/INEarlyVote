Updated: 2020-10-16

Total Early Votes: 591,499 (21.1% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 591,499 votes (63.3% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 271,686 in-person early votes (34.1% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 316,444 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 507,370 mail ballots.
