Indiana Early Voting Statistics

Updated: 2020-11-02

Total Early Votes: 1,711,662 (61.0% of 2016 turnout)

Total Ballots Cast Early

Hoosiers have cast 1,711,662 votes (183.2% of all votes cast early in 2016).

In-person Early Votes

Hoosiers have cast 1,212,939 in-person early votes (152.4% of all votes cast early in-person in 2016).

Returned Mail Ballots

Hoosiers have cast 487,724 ballots by mail, email, fax, or traveling board.

Mail Ballot Requests

Hoosiers have requested 537,736 mail ballots.
